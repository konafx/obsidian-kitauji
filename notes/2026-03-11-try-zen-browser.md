### shortcut
- sidebar
	- floating side bar: `Ctrl+Alt+S`
	- open/close side bar (compact mode trigger): `Ctrl+S`
- Switch space: `Alt+H` (default: `Ctrl+Alt+←`)
- Split View
	- New Split View: `Ctrl+Shift+*`
	- Close Split View: `Ctrl+Alt+U`
	- Switch split by  Holizontal: `Ctrl+Alt+H`
	- Switch split By Vertical: `Ctrl+Alt+V`
	- Switch Grid: `Ctrl+Alt+G`
- New Window(Spece): `Ctrl+N`
- New Tab: `Ctrl+T`
- Close Tab: `Ctrl+W`
- Close Window(Space): `Ctrl+Shift+W`
- Cycle Tab: none
